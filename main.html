kashmir

Kashmir is a scenic, mountainous region in the Himalayas, famed as "Paradise on Earth" for its lakes, meadows, and snow-capped peaks. Divided since 1947, it is contested by India (administering Jammu, Kashmir Valley, Ladakh), Pakistan (Azad Kashmir, Gilgit-Baltistan), and China (Aksai Chin). It remains a heavily militarized, flashpoint region.
